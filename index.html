🤖 AI Information & Disclaimer

Understanding how CrackAI's AI works — please read before use

AI Models Powering CrackAI

🧠 Smart AI (Text Engine): Handles all text-based questions across SSC and school curriculum. Powered by advanced Large Language Models (LLMs) optimised for Indian competitive examinations. Delivers fast, context-aware responses in English, Hindi, and Hinglish.

🔍 Vision AI (Multimodal Engine): Processes images, scanned documents, handwritten notes, and PDF files. Capable of reading question papers, MCQ options, diagrams, and printed text with high accuracy.

How AI Responses Are Generated

When you submit a question, it is securely transmitted to our AI engine, which generates a response based on patterns learned from vast educational datasets. The AI does not browse the internet in real time. Responses are generated fresh for each query and are not pre-written by human tutors.

Important Limitations — Please Read Carefully

⚠️ AI Can Make Errors: Despite high accuracy, AI-generated answers may contain factual mistakes, outdated information, or incorrect reasoning. Never submit an AI answer directly in an examination without verification.

⚠️ Not a Replacement for Official Materials: CrackAI is a study aid only. Official SSC notifications, NCERT textbooks, and government syllabus documents take absolute precedence over AI responses.

⚠️ GK & Current Affairs: The AI's knowledge has a training cutoff date. For current affairs, recent government schemes, and latest SSC notifications, always refer to authorised news sources and the official SSC website (ssc.gov.in).

⚠️ Mathematics & Reasoning: While the AI performs well on standard problems, complex calculations should be verified manually or using official answer keys.

Data Handling by AI Engine

Your questions and uploaded files are transmitted to our AI providers solely to generate your response. Content is not permanently stored by the AI engine and is not used to train AI models without explicit consent. All transmissions are encrypted via HTTPS.

No Liability for AI Outputs

CrackAI, its developers, and service providers expressly disclaim all liability for any academic, financial, or other harm arising directly or indirectly from reliance on AI-generated content. By using the Platform, you acknowledge that AI outputs are advisory in nature and not professionally certified educational guidance.

Responsible Use

Use CrackAI as a smart study companion — to understand concepts, check your thinking, and explore explanations. Always cross-reference important answers. The best results come from using AI alongside, not instead of, your official preparation materials.
